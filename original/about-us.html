Welcome to my website

Hi there! I am an undergraduate student at the Johns Hopkins University studying Cognitive Science and Computer Science. Some things I love include linguistics, my home state of Texas, warm weather, live music, spicy food and sweet treats. In between studying for classes, I enjoy discovering new music, experimenting with mixing songs on my DDJ-SB3 and writing every now and then! My passions and interests span a wide range of fields.

I used to feel this was a weakness compared to others who seemed to be a specialist in one subject. I wondered if I was too indecisive or spread too thin? My college experience led me to realize that my interdisciplinary nature can actually be a strength. I'm happy not being strictly confined to a particular focus in my studies and beyond. I hope my blog can show you that having multiple interests is not detrimental and that you can find success without compromising other things you care about as well.

More about the site...

Making a website was the first on my list of personal projects upon getting sent home from Hopkins back in March 2020 due to the COVID-19 epidemic. So, this site is the product of the extra time I found myself staying home, and finally learning how to create with HTML. In a software engineering class I took in the Spring of 2020, my team used a Bootstrap template for the web application we made, so I used one for the majority of this site. Aside from already being familiar with Bootstrap, another reason I decided to use it was because the design is also responsive on mobile devices. Therefore, my non-Bootstrap pages ones are not mobile-friendly, yet!

I have several pieces on food that I wrote in a class called, "Blogging on Food and Culture", that I need to upload. And, yes, this class was as cool as it sounds. I am also planning on creating some posts with advice that I learned while internship hunting and full-time recruiting. Keep an eye out for those and in the meantime, I hope you are staying safe and healthy.

- Daniela
