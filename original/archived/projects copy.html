Hire is a software project that I and a team of four other students worked throughout our Spring 2020 semester. The application was born out of a need for a more streamlined and organized system to conduct hiring for Course Assistants (CAs) for the Computer Science Department at Johns Hopkins. Our team worked in an agile-like environment to build the Java-based web app. The beta version is expected to be further tested by more staff members and students, with the goal of it being released department-wide in Spring 2021.

From the onset of our project, we knew that an intuitive and visually appealing user-interface was crucial. This is what sets our application apart from the current system (which involves multiple Google Forms and keeping manual tabs on each student's applications). Having an application where administrators can easily view CAs' qualifications and flag them throughout the steps of the hiring process drove our design.
